Brokerage oversight

Where brokerage supervision, licensing, and transaction oversight are required, users remain responsible for operating under the proper broker structure and within the scope of their authority.

  • FAST does not replace broker supervision, agency disclosure requirements, licensing rules, or office compliance procedures.
  • Users should ensure that representations about broker identity, authority, compensation, and agency are accurate in every transaction workflow.
  • When a broker of record or supervising broker must review or approve activity, that review should occur before the matter is treated as final.

Operational meaning

FAST can make a team faster, but it should never blur who is responsible, who is licensed, or who is supervising the work. Clear authority lines protect the deal and protect everyone involved in it.
