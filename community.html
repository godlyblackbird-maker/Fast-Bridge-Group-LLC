Rules & Regulations

Mandatory rules for everyone inside the FAST community workspace.

Rules & Regulations (Mandatory)

  • Respect is non-negotiable. All members must communicate with professionalism and respect at all times. Disrespect, harassment, or hostility will not be tolerated.
  • Stay on topic. This server is for business, meetings, and collaboration. Irrelevant conversations, spam, or off-topic messages are prohibited.
  • No interruptions during meetings. When meetings are in session, do not interrupt, talk over others, or create distractions. Wait your turn and contribute when appropriate.
  • No spam or self-promotion. Do not flood channels, send repeated messages, or promote unrelated services, products, or links without approval.
  • Confidentiality is required. Any business discussions, deals, or shared information must remain private. Leaking or sharing information outside this server is strictly prohibited.

Enforcement

Follow leadership instructions

Moderators and leadership decisions are final. Failure to comply will result in immediate action.

No inappropriate content

This includes offensive language, explicit material, or anything unprofessional. Keep everything clean and business-focused.

Be active and serious

This is a results-driven environment. If you are not here to contribute, learn, or execute, you do not belong here.

No trolling or disruptive behavior

Any attempt to disrupt the server, meetings, or members will result in immediate removal.

Zero tolerance enforcement

Failure to follow any of these rules will result in immediate removal from the server without warning.
