How this works

The right rail behaves like a Discord channel list. Click a room to swap the main panel without leaving the page, then jump into voice when you need live conversation instead of back-and-forth messaging.

  • Use announcements for pinned team updates.
  • Use deal rooms for active acquisition or disposition coordination.
  • Use resource drops for scripts, links, and call support material.
