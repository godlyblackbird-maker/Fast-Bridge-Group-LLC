Recommended Stack

Use one programmable carrier-grade provider as your main rail, a second as a fallback route, and a lightweight inbox layer only if your reps need a friendlier conversation view.

Primary rail: Twilio for A2P tooling, webhook control, and mature app integrations.
Backup rail: Telnyx for cost control and redundancy when volumes grow.
Rep inbox: Salesmsg if you want a simple team inbox without building one.

Copy-Ready SMS Blocks

First Touch

Hi [Agent First Name], this is [Your Name] with FAST BRIDGE GROUP. We are actively buying fixer and off-market homes in [Area] and can close fast. Any stale, pocket, or upcoming listings worth a look? Reply STOP to opt out.

Follow-Up

Checking back in, [Agent First Name]. We are still buying in [Area], especially probate, heavy fixer, and failed-escrow opportunities. If you want, I can text over our exact buy box and close timeline. Reply STOP to opt out.

Breakup / Keepalive

Last note from me for now, [Agent First Name]. If you ever have a seller who needs a quick as-is close in [Area], send it my way and I will respond fast. Happy to share proof of funds and our buy box when useful. Reply STOP to opt out.
