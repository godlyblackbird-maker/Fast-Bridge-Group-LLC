Conduct standards

Users are expected to operate in a way that protects consumers, respects fair housing rules, and follows brokerage supervision standards.

  • Discriminatory behavior, unlawful steering, improper solicitation, misleading statements, and deceptive practices may create serious licensing exposure.
  • FAST tools, templates, and workflows are meant to support disciplined operations, not justify conduct that violates law, policy, or broker instruction.
  • Any user handling leads, offers, listings, or investor conversations should assume that communications and actions may be scrutinized later.

Practical message

FAST can make users faster and more organized, but professional discipline still depends on judgment. Every call, text, email, disclosure decision, and property conversation should be handled in a way that would stand up to broker review, regulator review, and consumer review.
