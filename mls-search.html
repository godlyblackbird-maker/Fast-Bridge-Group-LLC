    Search Notes

    A few practical reminders so the search stays investor-focused.

    • Use broad location terms first. If you start with a narrow neighborhood, you can miss nearby pockets with stronger spreads.
    • Keep keyword clutter low. Two to five good investor signals usually beat a long, muddy string.
    • Save the brief, not just the result. The brief is what makes your process reusable across sites and across deals.
