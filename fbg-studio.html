Recording note

FBG Studio is a solid quick-capture option when you want to record a screen, camera, or mic feed directly in the browser without leaving this page. For longer sessions, heavier production work, or the most dependable screen recording setup, OBS Studio is the better option because it gives you stronger recording controls and a more robust desktop workflow. If you want a simpler browser-first alternative with quick sharing and lightweight recording tools, Screencastify is another solid option to consider.
