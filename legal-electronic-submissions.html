Submission responsibility

FAST can support electronic delivery and document handling, but the user remains responsible for the final submission standard required by the receiving agency, broker, or counterparty.

  • Users should verify file naming, signatures, attachment completeness, delivery method, deadline, and receipt or confirmation requirements.
  • Electronic convenience does not waive statutory timing, disclosure obligations, or agency-specific procedural rules.
  • When a filing or package is important, users should confirm that it was received, readable, and accepted by the intended recipient.

FAST workflow expectation

FAST is designed to help the team move faster on mobile and in the office, but every submission that carries legal or transaction consequences should be treated as a controlled final step. Speed should improve execution quality, not reduce it.
