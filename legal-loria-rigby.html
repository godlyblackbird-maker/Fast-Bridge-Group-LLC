Background & experience

Loria Rigby, founder of Rigby & Associates R.E. SVC, has practiced real estate for over 15 years after beginning her career in the industry as a broker's assistant while still working in retail management.

Before launching her own brokerage path, Loria spent more than 20 years in retail and worked her way into management. Even with that progress, she found the work unfulfilling and started pursuing real estate part time to build something more meaningful.

What began as supplemental income quickly became a calling. Helping people find the right home and guiding clients through real estate needs pushed her to leave retail behind and move fully into the business she now leads with long-term commitment and consistency.

What drives her work

Loria's work ethic is closely tied to her life story, family responsibilities, and a practical understanding of what clients need when a transaction carries real weight.

  • Born in Nicaragua and moved to the United States at age 13.
  • Grew up watching her mother work two jobs to support the family.
  • Raised two children as a single parent and understands the value of disciplined, reliable work.
  • Built her business through loyal clients, personal referrals, and repeat business.
  • Volunteers with TFCF, supporting homeless women and children.

Personal note

Outside of client work, Loria enjoys spending time with family, entertaining friends, and traveling. That balance shows up in the way she approaches brokerage work: personal, steady, and committed to meeting client needs without losing the human side of the transaction.
