Copy-ready Facebook custom disclaimer

Use the text below in Meta lead forms when you want a custom disclaimer in addition to the privacy-policy link.

By submitting this form, you agree that FAST BRIDGE GROUP may contact you by phone, email, and text message regarding your real-estate inquiry, property review, buying or selling opportunities, appointment scheduling, offer follow-up, and related marketing communications. Consent is not a condition of purchase. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out and HELP for help. Submitted information is subject to our Privacy Policy and Terms of Use.

What this disclaimer covers

  • Permission for FAST BRIDGE GROUP to follow up about the inquiry by call, email, or text.
  • Marketing opt-in language that makes clear follow-up may include promotional communication tied to the submitted request.
  • SMS notice basics including opt out, help instructions, and carrier-rate notice.
  • Cross-reference to the site's main privacy policy and terms pages.

Recommended use

This page is built for seller leads, buyer leads, cash-offer ads, listing intake, and similar real-estate campaigns where Facebook allows a custom disclaimer field. If the wording changes based on the campaign, keep the core consent, opt-out, and privacy-policy references intact.

Additional legal notice

Submission of a lead form does not create an agency relationship, guarantee an offer, promise eligibility, or create a binding transaction. Property evaluations, purchase discussions, timelines, and next steps depend on review, verification, and mutual agreement.

Fair housing and compliance

FAST BRIDGE GROUP supports equal housing opportunity and expects all lead handling, qualification, communication, and follow-up practices to comply with applicable fair housing, consumer protection, and advertising rules.
