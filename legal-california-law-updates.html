How to treat legal updates inside FAST

California law references inside FAST are operational support materials. They help users orient faster, but they do not replace the actual statute, regulation, MLS rule, broker instruction, or legal advice required for a final decision.

  • Real estate rules can change through legislation, regulatory amendments, enforcement interpretations, and MLS policy updates.
  • Users should review current source materials before relying on a summary for outreach, compliance, filings, advertising, transaction structure, or disclosure decisions.
  • Internal summaries are designed to speed up field judgment, training, and workflow awareness, not to stand alone as a legal authority.

FAST advantage

What makes FAST useful is that it brings deals, comps, and legal awareness into one operating environment. That helps agents and investors move with more clarity in the moment, but it also means users must keep their standards high and confirm the final rule before acting on a sensitive issue.
