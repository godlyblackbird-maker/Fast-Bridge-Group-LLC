Voicemail

Hey [Agent Name], this is [Your Name] with FAST BRIDGE GROUP. We are buying in [Area] right now and wanted to connect on anything off-market, stale, or fixer-heavy. Call or text me back when you get a chance.

  1. Keep the first sentence useful. Agents decide fast whether this is real deal flow or list spam.
  2. Ask for one concrete thing: stale listing, failed escrow, pocket opportunity, probate, or fixer inventory.
  3. State FAST BRIDGE GROUP clearly so the message feels traceable and professional.
