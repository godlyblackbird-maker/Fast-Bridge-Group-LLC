Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how FAST BRIDGE GROUP collects, uses, stores, and shares information submitted through our website, property inquiry forms, Facebook or Instagram lead ads, phone calls, text messages, and related real-estate marketing or acquisition workflows.

Effective date and scope

Effective date: April 8, 2026.

This policy applies to information collected by FAST BRIDGE GROUP through this website, landing pages, contact forms, seller or buyer inquiry forms, Facebook or Instagram lead forms, phone calls, emails, SMS conversations, and related advertising campaigns used to generate real-estate leads.

Information we collect

Depending on how you contact us, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, property address, city, buying or selling timeline, property details, investment or financing interest, preferred contact method, and any message or documents you choose to submit.

We may also collect technical and campaign information such as IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, referring ad source, form submission time, and lead-source metadata tied to our advertising platforms.

How lead information is collected

  • Directly from you when you fill out a form on our website or a lead form on Facebook or Instagram.
  • When you call, text, email, or otherwise communicate with FAST BRIDGE GROUP.
  • Automatically through cookies, analytics tools, server logs, and advertising attribution tools that help us understand campaign performance.
  • From follow-up conversations about buying, selling, listing, investing, or requesting a property review.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your inquiry and contact you about selling, buying, listing, or evaluating real estate opportunities.
  • To schedule calls, appointments, walkthroughs, follow-up conversations, and transaction-related communications.
  • To review property details, underwriting inputs, lead quality, service requests, and acquisition opportunities.
  • To improve our website, forms, advertising campaigns, operations, and customer support.
  • To maintain records, prevent abuse, and comply with legal, regulatory, brokerage, MLS, or contractual obligations.

Advertising and remarketing

FAST BRIDGE GROUP may use lead and website activity information to measure ad performance, build audiences, understand campaign results, and improve future real-estate marketing. This can include use of platform tools made available by Meta, Google, or similar advertising providers.

We do not sell your personal information in the ordinary sense of handing your information to unrelated companies for their independent use. We may, however, use advertising and analytics tools that process lead and visit data on our behalf for campaign measurement and communication workflows.

SMS consent and messaging

When you expressly consent to receive text messages, FAST BRIDGE GROUP LLC may use the mobile number you provide to send property inquiry updates, offer follow-up, appointment scheduling messages, transaction-related notices, and limited marketing follow-up related to your request.

  • SMS consent is not shared with third parties or affiliates for their own marketing purposes.
  • Message frequency varies based on your inquiry and ongoing communication needs.
  • Message and data rates may apply based on your mobile carrier plan.
  • You may reply STOP at any time to opt out or HELP for assistance.

When we share information

We may share information with service providers and business partners that help us operate our website, CRM tools, advertising workflows, messaging systems, analytics, document storage, appointment handling, and transaction support.

We may also disclose information when reasonably necessary to comply with law, enforce our policies, protect rights or safety, investigate fraud or abuse, or support brokerage and regulatory compliance requirements.

Cookies, analytics, and tracking tools

Our website may use cookies, pixels, tags, session storage, local storage, analytics tools, and similar technologies to keep the site functioning, remember preferences, measure traffic, attribute leads, and understand how visitors interact with our marketing and property workflows.

Your browser settings may allow you to limit certain tracking technologies, but some site functions may work differently if those settings are disabled.

Data retention and security

We keep information for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to inquiries, manage relationships, maintain business records, support transactions, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and satisfy legal or operational obligations.

No website, storage system, or messaging workflow can guarantee absolute security. FAST BRIDGE GROUP uses reasonable administrative, technical, and operational safeguards, but you submit information at your own risk.

Your choices and privacy requests

You may contact FAST BRIDGE GROUP to request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information you have submitted, subject to identity verification and any legal, recordkeeping, transaction, or compliance obligations that require retention.

You may also opt out of marketing emails using the unsubscribe method in the message and opt out of SMS by replying STOP.

Children's privacy

Our website and services are intended for adults involved in real-estate inquiries, transactions, and business communications. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 through our lead-generation workflows.
