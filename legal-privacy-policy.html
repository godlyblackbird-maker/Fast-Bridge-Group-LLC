Information practices

FAST may collect account details, usage information, uploaded materials, communication records, and workflow data needed to operate the platform and support authorized users.

  • Information may be used to authenticate users, maintain records, improve features, troubleshoot issues, and support transaction-related workflows.
  • FAST does not treat uploaded or platform-generated information as public-facing content unless the workflow clearly requires distribution by the user.
  • Users should avoid uploading material they are not authorized to share and should protect credentials, devices, and access links.

Operational reality

No web platform can honestly promise zero risk, so the right standard is controlled use, good judgment, limited sharing, and prompt cleanup when something no longer needs to be stored. FAST is meant to support that discipline, not replace it.
